mushroom pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish featuring a crispy crust topped with tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and various toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and vegetables. Its combination of crunchy crust, tangy sauce, and gooey cheese makes it a universally loved meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil or to taste
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, rinsed and dried
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil and sesame oil.
  3. Brush onto pre-baked pizza crust, covering the entire surface.
  4. Stack spinach leaves, then cut lengthwise into 1/2-inch strips; scatter evenly over crust.
  5. Cover pizza with shredded mozzarella, and top with sliced mushrooms.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and edges are crisp.
